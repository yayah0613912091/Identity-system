Identification sytem

Enter your South African ID Number to discover if your birthday coincides with any important public holidays and decode your ID information.

Format: YYMMDD GSSS CAZ

  • YYMMDD: Date of birth
  • G: Gender (0-4: Female, 5-9: Male)
  • SSS: Citizenship status
  • C: 8 or 9 (South African citizen)
  • A: 0-9 (Usually 0)
  • Z: Control digit
